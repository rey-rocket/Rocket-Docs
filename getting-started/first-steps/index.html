    Step-by-Step Campaign CreationðŸš€

    Step 1: Access the Campaign ManagerðŸš€

    1. Log in as Administrator in your LMS
    2. Navigate to Rocket Rewards â†’ Campaign Manager
    3. Click "Create New Campaign"

    Step 2: Configure Basic SettingsðŸš€

    1

    Campaign Details

    Fill out the basic campaign information:

    • Name: "Welcome Campaign" or "Course Completion Rewards"
    • Description: Brief explanation of the campaign purpose
    • Start Date: When the campaign should begin
    • End Date: Campaign expiration (optional)
    2

    Trigger Conditions

    Define what actions will trigger rewards:

    • Event Type: Course completion
    • Conditions: Minimum grade or completion percentage
    • Frequency: Once per course per student
    3

    Reward Configuration

    Set up the reward details:

    • Points Value: 100 points (suggested starting value)
    • Bonus Conditions: Extra points for high grades
    • Limits: Maximum points per student per campaign

    Step 3: Review and ActivateðŸš€

    :material-alert: Important

    Review all settings carefully before activation. Some changes may not be possible after the campaign goes live.

    Final Review Checklist: - [ ] Campaign name and description are clear - [ ] Trigger conditions match your learning objectives - [ ] Point values align with your overall strategy - [ ] Budget impact is within acceptable limits - [ ] Start/end dates are correct

    Activate Campaign: 1. Click "Review Campaign" to see a summary 2. If everything looks correct, click "Save & Activate" 3. The campaign will become active immediately

    Your first campaign is now live! Students will start earning points as soon as they complete the specified activities.
